Vegan Tacos

About This Dish

Vegan Tacos were one of the first dishes Taylor ever made for me and it was an instant favourite

This dish can be varied with your own favourite toppings - feel free to experiment.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 10 - Soft or Hard Taco Shells
  • 1 packet - Taco Seasoning (Old el Paso is our favourite brand)
  • 1 jar - Doritos Salsa (Choose your own heat level)
  • 500g - Naturli Vegan Mince
  • Guacamole for two
  • 80g - Vegan Cheese (Sheese is our favourite brand)
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • 1/2 cup - Water
  • 1 tsp - Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Add mince and oil to a saucepan. Break apart and heat. Once heated, add Water and Taco Seasoning. Mix well and allow to simmer
  3. Prepare Guacamole according to this recipe
  4. Line a baking tray and fill with Hard Taco shells. Put in oven for 5 minutes or until golden. Soft Taco shells are microwaved for 30 seconds
  5. Grate the vegan cheese to personal preference
  6. Shred Lettuce
  7. Separate all toppings into individual bowls and serve

Tip: We normally stack in the following order: Shell, Mince, Salsa, Cheese, Lettuce, Guac!
