Guacamole

About This Dish

I used to think I made the best Guacamole and my friends loved it when I brought it out. After meeting Taylor, she took over as Guacamole queen. This is her recipe.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 Avocados
  • 0.5 Tomato
  • 0.5 Red Onion

Directions

  1. Peel and add Avocados to a bowl
  2. Crush the Avocados
  3. Finely dice the Tomatoa and Red Onion
  4. Add to Avocado bowl and mix
  5. All salt and pepper to taste

Tip: We normally stack in the following order: Shell, Mince, Salsa, Cheese, Lettuce, Guac!
